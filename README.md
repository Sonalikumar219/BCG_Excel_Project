Project Overview

This project involved a comprehensive analysis of 500+ marketing interactions to evaluate the effectiveness of different campaign strategies and communication channels.

The objective was to identify the highest-performing segments to optimize marketing spend and conversion rates for future initiatives.

Data Processing: Leveraged Power Query to clean and transform raw interaction records, ensuring data integrity for downstream analysis.

Performance Metrics: Calculated total sales and conversion metrics for each interaction to determine ROI.

Pivot Table Analytics: Developed multi-dimensional summaries to compare sales performance across campaign types (A vs. B) and customer categories (New vs. Existing).

Business Insights & Results

Channel Dominance: Identified Email as the primary revenue driver, contributing $7,909 (54%) of total sales across all campaigns.

Campaign Performance: Determined that Campaign B was the most effective, generating $8,790 in total revenue compared to $5,869 from Campaign A.

Customer Segment Analysis: Found that Campaign A was more effective at driving revenue from New Customers specifically through the Email channel ($2,124).
